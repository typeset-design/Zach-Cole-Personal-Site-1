Lyft 1K Club

Lyft created a program to honor its most tenured drivers - those who reached the 1,000-ride milestone - by gifting them a custom fleece with a Lyft 1K badge. Think of it kind of like Ryan Gosling's cobra jacket in Drive.

I worked on a team of three to design and develop a landing page to announce the 1K Club. Drivers who had already hit the milestone could request their fleece, and other drivers could check how many rides shy of 1,000 they are.

My amazing teammates Meghan and Mark created spectacular 3d-rendered animations to help bring this website to life.
